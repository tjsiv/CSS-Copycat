Fandom

Fandom started out with about 1,500 pages from a core group of enthusiastic fan contributors. Over 15 years later, Fandom encompasses over 40 million content pages in over 80 languages on 250,000 wikis about every fictional universe ever created. Fandom is where fans find themselves through entertainment. We provide a brand safe and judgement-free zone where people can discover new favorites, go as deep as they want to and engage with other fans. And we can do it across virtually any entertainment or gaming property out there.
